Professional Profile

Finding new techniques to optimize and expand user experience are what I find most satisfying about being a developer. Stretching the experience beyond a list of features or what the end-user sees makes for a more rewarding and enjoyable role as a dev.

I’m a fan of modular and easily testable code, and prefer readability and explicitness over unnecessary magic. I also favor more functional-derived patterns such immutability, composition, and avoiding side effects when possible.

Experimenting with small projects, as well as working on ways to enhance my tooling and habits for productivity are ways in which I like to spend my free time. I get a thrill out of learning new things, and I tend to geek out over discovering new frameworks, libraries and tools.
